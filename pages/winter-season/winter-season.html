SURF LESSON

The Oaxaca Cost is a Wild beauty, nature still strongly over tops this area, offering a mystical and unique connection with the scenary, its a very special place to take your first surf lesson or improve your surfing abilities



INCLUDE

Transportation 4x4 (pick up and drop of)

Equipment (surfboard, leash, rash, guard)

Passionate, qualified and experience
