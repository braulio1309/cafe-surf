OAXACA COAST

Southern Oaxaca is the world’s best right-hand point breaks waves. With us you will be on a search for the best waves according to the weather conditions and your surfing level.

Café Surf is a company formed by Gil & his guides, all of them are from this area and have a lot of experience and knowledge about the southern Oaxacan coastline, the surf breaks, and the communities in general. Our mission is offering you the best attention, Gil and his team will plan every tour day by day according the customers surf level and the weather condition using is expertise to find the best waves of the day.
