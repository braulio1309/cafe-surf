OAXACA COAST

Southern Oaxaca is the world’s best right-hand point breaks waves. With us you will be on a search for the best waves according to the weather conditions and your surfing level.

Café Surf is a company formed by Gil & his guides, all of them are from this area and have a lot of experience and knowledge about the southern Oaxacan coastline, the surf breaks, and the communities in general. Our mission is offering you the best attention, Gil and his team will plan every tour day by day according the customers surf level and the weather condition using is expertise to find the best waves of the day.

Nice accommodation in a gated residence in a full furnished condominium with WIFI, ACs, pool

We always install our group in selected, nice full & furnished condominiums with ample spaces to allow you to stretch and which is essential with good bed mattresses. Cooking service (Breakfast, Lunch & Diner, READ MORE 3 x meals per day) - food included

Don’t worry about your meals - breakfast, lunch & diner will be taking care by our cooking team. The quality of the food is important for us, it needs to be fresh and tasty. You will discover an authentic oaxacan cuisine. Depending on your arrival time, the cook would have prepared some fresh guacamole with ceviche and a pico de gallo for you. If necessary, we can adapt the culinary preferences (allergies, vegetarians, etc.) which must be specified before your arrival. Food will be always ready upon your arrival from surfing, the breakfast will be prepared the night before in order to make your early wake up easier… just push the coffee machine button and smell the nice flavor of the Oaxacan coffee, open the fridge and grab your orange juice, your fruits plates and the dishes that the cook prepared for you in advance. * The food is included – alcohol and sodas are extra.

Fruits, snacks (baguettes) & water provided during surf sessions

If the conditions are right, we install our camp and surf until conditions change, usually early afternoon. We like to do a long session, come back to Huatulco.
